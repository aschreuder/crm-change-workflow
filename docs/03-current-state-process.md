Current process:
1. Requester submits a spreadsheet, email or slack change request. (usually unstructured)
2. CRM Administrator checks the fields manually.
3. Missing details cause back and forth.
4. Administrator updates records.
5. Manager may or may not approve (Long feedback loop)
6. Evidence remains across email, slack and spreadsheet - no audit logging

Pain points:
- Change requests come from multiple sources and are usually unstructured.
- CRM admin wastes time by checking the fields manually.
- Time loss due to back and forth communications.
- Bottleneck when having to wait for manager approval.
- No audit logging
- Limited status updates
